How to Play

1. Create or Join — One player creates a game and shares the 4-letter room code. The other player enters the code to join.

2. Choose Your Side — Pick Pirates or Navy. Each faction has unique ship designs!

3. Place Your Fleet — Drag ships from the dock onto the grid. Click placed ships to pick them up. Right-click to rotate in place. You have 10 ships total.

4. Battle! — Take turns firing at the enemy grid. Click a cell to fire.

5. Hit = Extra Turn — If you hit an enemy ship, you get to fire again! Miss and it's your opponent's turn.

6. Sink All Ships — Destroy all 10 enemy ships to win. Ships are sunk when all their cells are hit.

7. Timer — If a turn timer is set, you must fire before time runs out or your turn is skipped!
